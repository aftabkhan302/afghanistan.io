About Iqra Schooling System

Established in 1999, Iqra Schooling System has been a beacon of quality education, combining modern academic excellence with strong Islamic values. Our mission is to produce confident, knowledgeable, and morally upright citizens.

We offer state-of-the-art facilities, smart classrooms, a dedicated computer lab, science laboratories, a library, and a prayer area. Our qualified teachers ensure each child receives personalized attention.

Our Motto: "Read! In the name of your Lord" — Inspiring a love for learning and faith.
